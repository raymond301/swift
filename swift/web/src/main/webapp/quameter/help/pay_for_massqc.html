How To Pay For MassQC

To sign up for MassQC , go here and request a 30-day evaluation key and download MassQC. After you have successfully evaluated MassQC , if you decide to purchase MassQC , you simply contact our sales department and request a quote. Once your quote is approved by your institution, you can submit a Purchase Order (PO) to us and we will send you the invoice and a permanent MassQC key.
